The Artist

Lagcity Keys
Afro Hip-Hop · Nigeria

LAGCITY
KEYS

Lagcity Keys is a Lagos Island-born Nigerian Afro Hip-Hop artist signed to Oddacieux Records, crafting music that bridges the grit of the streets with the elegance of Afrobeats. His debut project Too Little Too Much announced him as a force to be reckoned with — telling stories of resilience, identity, and ambition that resonate across Nigeria and the diaspora.

From Lagos Island to the world, Lagcity Keys delivers bars and melodies with conviction. His next chapter — the EP The Devil Picks His Favourites — is set to drop in June 2026.
